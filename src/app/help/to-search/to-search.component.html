1. Click on the Create Search button above.
  2. On the Create an Expression page, you may leave the field and operator set to (automatic) (automatic).
  3. Start typing a search term.
  4. After entering 3 or more characters, the AutoComplete displays any matching values.
  5. Click on a desired AutoComplete result to complete your expression and display the Expression matches ... page.
  6. Do any one of:
    • Press Enter.
    • Click on the Get Results button below the list.
    • Click on the Results button above.
  7. Your results will display.
    • If your search resulted in only one substance, its Substance page will open.
    • Otherwise, the Summaries List page will open.
      • You may navigate through the paged results to find substances of interest.
      • Click any substance's link to open its Substance page.
        • Then click on the Results button to return to the Summaries List where you left off.
