Each expression will usually have a Search Field, an Operator, and a Value. The following fields and operators are available, though not all operators are available for each field. In general, the "auto" field and operator should not be used; it takes more processing and you also may not get back the matches you wanted. The "auto" field and operator exist primarily for the autocomplete functionality. Multi-expression searches may not include "auto" fields or operators.
