Each expression will usually have a Search Field, an Operator, and a Value. The following fields and operators are available, though not all operators are available for each field. In general, the (automatic) field and (automatic) operator should not be used with the API; they take more processing and you also may not get back the matches you wanted. The (automatic) field and (automatic) operator exist primarily for the AutoComplete functionality. Multi-expression searches may not include the (automatic) field or (automatic) operator. Likewise, the regex operator may not be used with the (automatic) field.
